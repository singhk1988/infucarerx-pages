Virtual Onboarding:

Includes the practice of health care delivery, care management, plan of treatment discussion, history and physical forms, assessments, transfer of medical data, and education using audio, video, or data communications.

Records:

The Virtual Onboarding sessions with you will not be recorded or stored. The nurse case manager will document your information in your medical record consistent with applicable laws.

Digital Health Information:

The health information related to a person’s medical history, including symptoms, diagnoses, procedures, and outcomes. A health record includes information such as: a patient’s history, lab results, X-rays, clinical information, demographic information, notes, and others that may will be discussed with a nurse case manager during the Virtual Onboarding assessment.
